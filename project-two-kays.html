Brand Story 2024

Two Kay's Flower Farm

Kalispell, MT

Two Kay's Flower Farm is a family flower farm in Kalispell, MT — and Crystal's vision for it goes way beyond selling bouquets. She was building something bigger: a marketing initiative inviting people into a simpler, more intentional way of living. The farm as a destination. The flowers as a lifestyle.

She brought me in across three farm events to capture that story. The video was the main thing — the photography was the cherry on top.

Client Two Kay's Flower Farm
Location Kalispell, MT
Services Brand Video
Event Coverage
Brand Photography
Year 2024

The Brief

Invite people into the life, not just the product

Crystal wasn't just selling flowers — she was building a brand around the simplistic Americana lifestyle her farm represents. She needed content that made people feel something when they saw it. The kind of video that makes you want to slow down, show up, and be part of it.

Over three farm events in Kalispell, I was there to capture it — the light, the people, the pace of the place. The goal was a brand film that felt less like marketing and more like an invitation.

The Work

Three events. One story.

Across three separate farm events, I captured the full scope of what Crystal was building — the community she was drawing in, the beauty of the land, and the quiet intentionality behind everything Two Kay's does. The brand film was the centerpiece. Photos supported the larger campaign.

"Working with Joshua was a great experience — he took the time to understand our vision and was genuinely invested in capturing the little moments that make our flower farm so special. The final project was everything we hoped for and truly reflects the heart of what we do."
— Crystal Kay Allison, Two Kay's Flower Farm
