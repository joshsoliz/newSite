Motion Design 2024

Easter Promo

Fresh Life Church

39 seconds to make someone feel like Easter Sunday is somewhere they need to be. That's the job of a church promo — not just to inform, but to create anticipation. Motion design is the right tool for that. It's fast, it's flexible, and when it's done right it makes even a short piece feel like an event.

This one was built entirely in motion — no live footage, just design, type, and animation doing the heavy lifting. The constraint made it interesting.

Client Fresh Life Church
Services Motion Design
Kinetic Typography
Sound Design
Year 2024
Length 0:39

The Brief

Make Easter feel like something worth showing up for

The brief was to create a short promo that could run on screens, social, and anywhere else the church needed to generate awareness for their Easter services. Short runtime, big energy — and it had to feel cohesive with the church's existing visual identity.

For churches running motion graphics on screens during services, this is exactly the kind of piece that sets the tone before anyone gets up to speak.

The Work

39 seconds that do their job

The whole piece runs on timing. Motion design at this length has to be tight — every frame is earning its place. The type treatment, the pacing, the music sync — it all has to work together or it falls apart fast.

"Church motion design doesn't need to be flashy. It needs to feel like the church made it — not like a template someone bought."
